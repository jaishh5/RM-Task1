ABOUT US

Jaishnav's & Amy's July wedding doesn't need many words. The two of them had a hip VW Bulli, great guests, perfect weather and a wonderful wedding. What more do you want! After the ceremony in the old port authority in Dortmund we went over to the beautiful Tyde, which is located directly on the canal. You have chosen to get married exactly as you are. Natural, uncomplicated, in a good mood and unconventional! I am admittedly an absolute fan of personal, authentic and, above all, warm weddings, which is exactly what I was able to experience on that day.

Pictures say more than a thousand words, so now I wish you all a lot of fun watching!

A day that was so pleasantly cordial, honest and just beautiful that I will certainly remember it for a very long time. I thank you for that!
